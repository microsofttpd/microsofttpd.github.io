This channel is open to all in the Microsoft Dynamics partner and customer community, and get updates on latest news and updates in the following Microsoft Dynamics technology areas
<ul>
	<li>Dynamics AX</li>
	<li>Dynamics CRM</li>
	<li>Dynamics GP</li>
	<li>Dynamics NAV</li>
</ul>
